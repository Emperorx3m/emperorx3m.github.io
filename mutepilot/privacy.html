Privacy Policy for MutePilot

1. Introduction

Welcome to MutePilot ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MutePilot handles user information when you use our mobile application ("app").

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

MutePilot does not collect, store, or share any of the following data:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email, phone number)
  • User account data
  • Contacts, photos, or messages
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

3. Subscription & Payment Information

We use React Native IAP to manage subscriptions on Google Play and Apple App Store. All payments are processed securely via the respective stores.

We do not access or store your payment details or subscription history. Any billing issues or cancellations are handled directly by:

  • Google Play Terms
  • Apple App Store Terms

4. Location Usage

If you choose to use features that require location (e.g., map-based selection), the app may:

  • Access your device’s location using system-level permissions
  • Use location only for in-app display and selection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share your location with any third party.

5. Calendar Usage

MutePilot may request access to your device’s calendar to read event data. This data is used solely to help you set automated muting rules based on your calendar events. No calendar data is collected, stored externally, transmitted, or shared with any third party. All processing happens securely on your device.

6. App Permissions

MutePilot requires certain system permissions (including location and calendar access) strictly to provide its core functionality. We request only those permissions needed for features to work as intended. We do not use these permissions for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the app’s essential operation.

7. Analytics and Tracking

MutePilot does not use:

  • Analytics SDKs (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reporting tools (e.g., Crashlytics)
  • Advertising or tracking libraries

8. Third-Party Links or Services

The app may allow you to open third-party services like Google Maps in an in-app browser. MutePilot does not track your activity on these platforms.

Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

9. Data Security

As we do not collect or store any user data, no personal data is at risk of being compromised. Your data remains on your device.

10. Children’s Privacy

MutePilot is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted within the app or via app store release notes.
